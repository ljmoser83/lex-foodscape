Lexington, KY Foodscape Mapping Project

The goal of the Lexington, KY Foodscape Mapping Project is to design an interactive web map that explores the foodscape concept and investigates the prevalence and location of various types of food options in Lexington, Kentucky. This project will visualize the food choices that we are faced with in our daily lives. These choices and exposures play a large role in determining how we consume food and the overall health impact of our consumption decisions.

Map users will gain a better understanding of the Lexington, KY foodscape. Gaining this understanding will raise awareness of the daily choices we face in obtaining nourishment and a healthy diet. An underlying theme of this project is a focus on improving food system equity (equal access and exposure to healthy food choices across demographic and socioeconomic groups).

Tutorial

Enter the "Map Tools" tab and add a location of interest by one of three methods:

  1. Enter an address to be geocoded
  2. Click the "Use Current Location" button
  3. Click on a point of interest on the map

The location of interest will be buffered by the user determined normal mobility radius. This area of interest will represent the user's foodscape.

An unhealthy/healthy food choice ratio will be calculated for the area of interest. This ratio will indicate the relative exposure of consumers to healthy and unhealthy food choices within the area of interest. An area with high exposure to unhealthy food options is any area with a ratio greater than 4:1.

The "Locate Nearest Healthy Food Option" tab and associated button can be used to determine the location of the nearest healthy food option to the location of interest.
